Where we stayed

For this trip we stayed at the Sonesta Resort. I had never stayed here, but it's safe to say I will definitely be back in the future! The rooms were spacious, the property was gorgeous and so clean , and the staff was very friendly. We grabbed breakfast every morning at Heyward's , the hotel's restaurant. Definitely get the almond french toast, it was incredible! Every morning we would take our coffee to go & relax in one of the hammocks and enjoy the gorgeous views!
